## Overview

EzBiz Services automates your entire front office, handling calls, texts, scheduling, follow-ups, reviews, and reporting. You focus on delivering great service while the system manages customer interactions 24/7. Built specifically for service businesses, it prevents missed opportunities and scales with your growth.

No contracts, a 30-day money-back guarantee, and live setup in 24 hours make it risk-free to start.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account at `https://dashboard.example.com/signup` and choose your plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Phone" icon="phone">
    Forward your business number to the EzBiz system via the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Integrations" icon="settings">
    Connect your calendar and enable webhooks for notifications.

````javascript
// Example webhook for incoming calls
app.post('/webhook/calls', (req, res) => {
  const { callerNumber, transcription } = req.body;
  // Send SMS alert
  sendSMS(`New call from ${callerNumber}: ${transcription}`);
  res.status(200).send('OK');
});
````
