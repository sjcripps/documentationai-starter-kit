## Overview

Sign up and create your first project to start managing documentation with EzBiz.

## Steps

1. Go to `https://dashboard.ezbizservices.com`  
2. Click "Sign up" and enter your details  
3. Confirm your email address  
4. Login to your dashboard  
5. Click "New Project" to begin  

You're all set to organize your docs with EzBiz!
