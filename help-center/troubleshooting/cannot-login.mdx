---
title: Cannot Login to Dashboard
description: Troubleshoot dashboard login issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page reloads
- No error message
- Cannot access dashboard

## Causes

- Incorrect credentials
- Browser cache issue

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    Click "Forgot password" on the login page and follow instructions.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Try logging in again after clearing browser cache/cookies.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
